In 2020, I came across a Portuguese project named Trojan Was Unicorn (THU) and accepted the challenge of creating its digital community version.

THU is a distinctive global community of artists and creators that began as a small group gathering for an IRL event 15 years ago and has since become a reference point in the digital entertainment industry.

The challenge

Develop an online application that will serve as the foundation for engaging and growing a community centered around an annual in-person event.

We conducted a series of workshops and brainstorming sessions, which led to the following main points:

  • THU is a dynamic and vibrant community; our online presence should reflect this.
  • We aim to expand our initiatives to include online-only events, particularly in response to the COVID-19 pandemic.
  • The application will serve as the community's central hub and help attract more members.
  • The final user experience (UX) should incorporate these key ideas and be creative, bold, and unique.

The information architecture

We tried and tested various options, and it was clear that we needed to create pages to drive attention to each area.
